# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Positive: empathy and kindness, respect for differing views, graceful acceptance of feedback.
Unacceptable: harassment, insults, personal or political attacks, publishing others' private information.

## Enforcement
Report unacceptable behavior to thakiaiplatform@gmail.com. Complaints are reviewed promptly and fairly, respecting reporter privacy.
